Hush Tab - effortless control of your background music volume while browsing the web.

When you click the current displaying tab will be put under hush control.

When a tab is under hush control the icon will change to

The tab under hush control will get this blue "monitor" icon:

Whenever another tab starts playing sound the hush controlled tab will be automatically muted.

When no other tabs are making sound the hush controlled tab will be un-muted again.
